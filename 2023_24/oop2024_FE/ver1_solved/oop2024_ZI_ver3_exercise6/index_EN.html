In this task you are modeling classes, interfaces, and enumerations for vehicles management (Vehicle).

There are three types of vehicles: Car, Truck, and Motorcycle and no other types of vehicles could be created.

Every vehicle has model(String), power (int) and regularPrice(double). Define getters and setters for all of these attributes.

An uniqueness of cars is that they have emission standard (euroStd:string). Trucks has max load (maxLoad:int), and motorcycles have their type (type:MotorcycleType) which could be one of the followings: Chopper, Sport, Racing or OffRoad.

Class Vehicle should have the method

public double getPrice(boolean isVIP)

that either returns regular price (for non-VIP customers) or discounted price for VIP customers. The discount varies based on the vehicle type. Discount for motorcycles is 15%, for cars 10%, and for trucks 5%.

Cars, and motorcycles can be rented (i.e. Rentable). There may be other rentable things beside cars, and motorcycles. Rentable should define the following method

public double getRentingPricePerDay()

For cars and motorcycles, renting price per day is 0.1% of their prices for VIP customers.

Upon finishing tasks elaborated above, finish the method rentPrice in the class PriceCalculator. The method should return renting price per day for all rentable vehicles for the collection.

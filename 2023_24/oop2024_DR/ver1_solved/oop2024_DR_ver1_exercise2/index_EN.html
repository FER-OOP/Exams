In this task you are modeling classes, interfaces, and enumerations for games equipment management (Equipment).

There are three types of equipment: Pinball, MiniGolf, and Billiard and no other types of equipment could be created.

Every equipment has name(String), and regularPrice(double).

An uniqueness of pinballs is that they have manufacturer and genre (manufacturer:string, genre:string). Minigolf has number of holes (holes:int), and billiard have their type (type:BilliardType) which could be one of the followings: Standard, Snooker, or Carom, and flag if they are coin operated or not (coinOperated:boolean)

Class Equipment should have the method

public double getPrice(boolean isVIP)

that either returns regular price (for non-VIP customers) or discounted price for VIP customers. The discount varies based on the equipment type. Discount for billiards is 15%, for pinballs 10%, and for minigolfs 5%.

Pinballs, and billiards can be rented (i.e. Rentable). There may be other rentable things beside pinballs, and billiards. Rentable should define the following method

public double getRentingPricePerDay()

For pinballs and billiards, renting price per day is 0.1% of their prices for VIP customers.

Define getters and setters for all of these attributes. Note: Getter for boolean variable should be prefixed with is instead of get.

Upon finishing tasks elaborated above, finish the method rentPrice in the class PriceCalculator. The method should return renting price per day for all rentable equipment in the collection.

Note: do not write constructors for the classes.
